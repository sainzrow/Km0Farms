Introduction

The farming called Km Zero is a filosofy of sustainable consumption: farms are opening doors to customers allowing them to buy their own stuff without having to go through intermediaries.
This phenomenon known as "Farmers Market" is becoming increasingly popular in Italy and in the world.
It is contrasting with the globalization because that products Km Zero, by definition, can't "travel" so much, and, to be such, they can't exceede 70Km from the farming place.
The establishment of that kind of commercial distribution is the subject, nowadays in legislation, although in many Regions, like Veneto, have already approved the law that manages foods Km Zero.
There are many advantages for this kind of market:
- economic: the absence of intermidiates and the lack of the road trasport make prices lower at least by 30%.
- enviormental: the very low packages and the lack of the transport make the CO2 nearly by 0, also they increase the road safety because of the minor veichles travelling on road.
- Better product quality: not having to be packaged, labeled, distributed and sold, they are still fresh with the certainty whether it is food national, regional or rather, what now quite rare, especially with something that regards to fruit and vegetables, which is often coming from the other side of the world;
- sustainability: in terms of support to local communities.
- Then there is also an interesting cultural aspect: the approach of the consumer to the rural reality allows a direct contact with the nature, animals, the earth and the knowledge of the products we offer, giving more meaning to the passing of the season s that we offer fruit and vegetables are always different.
Unfortunately it is not easy to make an expenditure "complete" according to our standards of spoiled consumers used to having everything and always a lot of products agriculture are related to climatic zones and territory, but the multiple distribution channels agriculture Km Zero, tempt and help everyone involved.
So you can find the products at the farms, village markets, mechanical distributors working by coins, markets specially created or finally, calling on the GAS, the joint purchasing groups.

How does it works?

This project is offered as a search engine that allows the end-user to find out the main informations about the farming and enviormental Km Zero.
This web application is based on many opensource resources as Google Maps, DataSets where are lstored the informations grouped by districs of the Regione Veneto.
